Offline Portfolio Instructions (Step 16)
=======================================

How to open offline
-------------------
1. Download and extract the offline ZIP bundle.
2. Double-click `index.html` (root of the folder) to open the site in any modern desktop browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari).

What still requires an internet connection
-----------------------------------------
- YouTube embeds/links (Videos section, Evidence Map, etc.).
- Formspree-powered contact/feedback forms (Testimonials + Contact sections cannot submit without internet).
- External links to GitHub, Credly, LinkedIn, and other third-party resources.

Where to find key evidence offline
----------------------------------
- CV PDFs: `assets/pdf/Aristide_Katagaruka_CV_ENG.pdf`, `assets/pdf/Aristide_Katagaruka_CV_FRE.pdf`, `assets/pdf/Aristide_Katagaruka_CV_ENG&FRE.pdf`.
- Certificates & score reports: `assets/pdf/certificates/` (e.g., AZ-900, MOS scores).
- Course notes and proofs: `assets/pdf/course-notes/` and `assets/pdf/course-proofs/`.
- Technical documentation report: `assets/pdf/reports/multi-cloud-technical-report.pdf`.
- Tutor reports & validations: `assets/pdf/tutor-reports/`.

Navigation tips offline
-----------------------
- All internal sections (Home, Services, Skills, Portfolio, etc.) and anchor links work without internet.
- Project detail pages remain under the `projects/` folder—links from the Portfolio grid open those HTML files locally.
- PDF evidence links open directly in the browser’s built-in PDF viewer.
